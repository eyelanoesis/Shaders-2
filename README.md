# About Rune Abro & Hackstage

This repository contains information summarizing public-facing details about Rune Abro and the creative collective Hackstage.

## Rune Abro

Rune Abro is a Copenhagen-based multidisciplinary artist, performer, new-media content creator, and scholar. His work is characterized by a creative and unconventional approach that blends abstract art, storytelling, music, photography, and philosophy.

-   **Role**: Integral part of the Hackstage creative collective.
-   **Background**: Music, Photography, Philosophy (University of Copenhagen).
-   **Online Presence**: Active on platforms like YouTube and Vimeo.

## Hackstage

Hackstage is described as a creative "hivemind" or collective based in Copenhagen. Their work focuses on producing digital art, multimedia experiences, and experiential storytelling.

-   **Mission**: To bend perceptions and challenge conventional ideas of reality.
-   **Output**: Avant-garde art and creative technology projects.
